Your Personalized Recommendations

Based on your latest assessment, we’ve selected simple strategies to support your mental well-being. These tips are categorized by what you may be experiencing. Try the ones that feel right for you.

❤️ For Tension or Feeling Overwhelmed

  • Schedule just 5 minutes to pause and do nothing.
  • Try a “brain dump” write down everything on your mind.
  • Gently stretch your neck, shoulders, and back.

💛 For Anxiety or Restlessness

  • Try box breathing: Inhale for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 4.
  • Write down what you can control right now.
  • Unplug from your phone for 20 minutes and listen to calming sounds.

💛 For Anxiety or Restlessness

  • Write down 3 small wins from today—even if it’s just “I got out of bed.”
  • Try a 10-minute walk in fresh air.
  • Call or text someone you trust and say hello.

These recommendations are here to guide you but you don’t have to do everything at once. Choose what feels helpful today, and come back when you’re ready.
